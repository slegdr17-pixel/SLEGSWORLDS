HAPPY 5TH ANNIVERSARY

I crawled from the ruins of a betrayed kingdom,
Heavy with the dust of old wars.
I walked with ghosts in the twilight mist,
My heart a locked vault, afraid of the light.

Then, you built a fortress from a single glance.
Heaven saw my fractured soul,
And sent a holy grace I did not deserve.
Yet, storms still rage from my blinded fears.

My soul still aches from the poison of the past.
In the dark, my old scars echoed—
And I lashed out, hurting the one I love.
But day by day, I just fall deeper.

Into your abyss, where my terrors finally erase.
We are bound by the anchor of the Holy Spirit.
Tonight, my eyes look to heaven,
And I choose to trust in you again.

Our battles, our scars, and this beautiful love—
I leave them safely in God’s holy hands.
